Seven Layer Casserole

The recipe I chose was the Seven Layer Cassorole. My family and I have been having this for dinner for a couple months now. We started off having it for dinner on normal days and we still do but over time we have started making it for guests and family members. It is a good dish to make for people who come over because it has a lot of different components so if someone doesn't like a specific food, there are other parts of the dish they may enjoy. Last summer, we made it for my aunt who doen't eat meat. Because it is a dish that we can easily adjust to dietary restrictions, we were able to make her portion without meat and she still expressed to us that it was "A very delicious meal." I think it is a convienient dish because a lot of the ingredients are things you can find pre packaged in cans, boxes, or bags at the grocery store. It is time efficiant and all you have to do is warm up those ingredients and add them to the cassorole. Overall it is a somewhat recent addition to my family's meal plan and we enjoy having it.
