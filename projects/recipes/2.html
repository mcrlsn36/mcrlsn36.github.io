Homemade Mac and Cheese

The second dish I chose is Mac and Cheese. I have liked to eat mac and cheese since I was very little. It has been my favorite food my whole life ans there are very few mac and cheese recipes that I dislike. My favorite way to eat it is definetly homemade. My family often has homemade mac and cheese on thanksgiving or christmas. We also find ourselves eating it for dinner on random days of the week sometimes. My mom who doesn't really like mac and cheese likes this recipe and says that "it tastes high quality."

Prepearation time: 40min Serving size: 6 servings
Ingredients Amount Instructions
Dry elbow macaroni 1 1/2 cups Preheat oven to 350 degrees, bring a pot of water to a boil and add the pasta. Follow the insructions on the pasta box for cook time.
Butter or margarine 3 tablespoons Melt the butter in a pot large enough to hold the pasta when it's done.
All purpose flour 3 tablespoons Add the flour and stir it on medium heat until mixture is lightly browned(1-2 min)
Milk 2 cups Add the milk and wisk until there is no lumps. Then add the salt and pepper.
Salt and Pepper 1/2 teaspoon Cook over medium heat until sauce is thick and start to bubble (6 minutes).
Sharp chedder cheese 1 cup Stir in the cheese and wisk until it is smooth and melted.
Swiss cheese 1 cup
When pasta is done, drain it and add it into the sauce. Then stir in the pasta and bake in oven until brown and bubbly (20-25) minutes.

Tips/Substitutions

  • If you are not a big fan of elbow macaroni, you can use a different kind of pasta.
  • I would recomend hand-grading the blocks of swiss and chedder cheese as it inhances the flavor of the sauce more than bagged, pre-shredded cheese.
  • It is always a good idea to taste it as you go as you may decide that it needs more of a specific ingredient.
  • Don't be afraid of seasoning! It may come as a suprise but the addition of the salt and pepper can really enhance the flavor of the mac and cheese. Add a little, stir it, and try it to make sure there is enough.
  • If you like a different kind of cheese like parmasean, you can add that in to your liking.
