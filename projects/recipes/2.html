Homemade Mac and Cheese

The second dish I chose is Mac and Cheese. I have liked to eat mac and cheese since I was very little. It has been my favorite food my whole life. There are very few mac and cheese recipes that I dislike. My favorite kind is definetly homemade. My family often has homemade mac and cheese on thanksgiving or christmas. We also find ourselves eating it for dinner on random days of the week sometimes. My mom who doesn't really like mac and cheese likes this recipe and says that "the graded cheese makes it taste higher quality."
